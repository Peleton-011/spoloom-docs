Spoloom dev docs

Intro

Welcome to the Spoloom development documentation. This repository serves as a comprehensive guide for developers working on Spoloom and for anyone working on any peripheral field.

Here, you will find detailed information about the system architecture, codebase, APIs, and best practices to ensure efficient and consistent development.

The goal is to provide clear, up-to-date, and easily accessible documentation to streamline the development process, the colaboration and enhance your understanding of the Spoloom platform.

Whether you're a developer or just working alongside, this documentation will be your trusted companion as you contribute to the Spoloom project and will facilitate the communication amongst the team.
